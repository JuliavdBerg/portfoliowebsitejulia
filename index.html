PartyGheists - school group project

Bash your way through up to four of your friends in this fast-paced party game where no one is safe from the fall. Find powerful items to help you bump, smash, or blow your friends up as you try to be the last one standing on the floating island where the ghosts roam. You have it out for everyone, but so do they, so be on your guard because any bump could be your last!
